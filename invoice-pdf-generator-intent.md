# Invoice & PDF Generator — Product Intent

**Type:** Web tool  
**Market:** Freelance product · High demand · Low competition

---

## The Problem

Small businesses — freelancers, contractors, consultants, shops — need to send professional invoices and quotes every week. Most use Word/Excel templates that look unprofessional, or pay $20–$50/month for tools like FreshBooks or QuickBooks just to generate a PDF. They want something simple, free to use, and branded with their own logo and colors — with no monthly fee.

---

## The Solution

A clean web tool where a business owner fills in their details once, adds line items, and downloads a pixel-perfect branded PDF in one click. No login required for the basic version. No subscription. Their brand, their invoice, instantly.

### Core Features
- Invoice generation
- Quote / estimate builder
- Logo & brand colors upload
- One-click PDF download
- Auto tax & total calculation
- Send PDF via email

---

## Who Buys This (Target Clients on Upwork / Fiverr)

- **Tradespeople** — plumbers, electricians, builders who want to send professional quotes fast from their phone or laptop
- **Freelancers** — designers, writers, developers who invoice clients monthly and want it to look professional
- **Small shops & agencies** — boutiques, marketing agencies, consultants who don't want to pay SaaS fees

---

## Tech Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|---|---|
| Frontend | Next.js + React |
| PDF engine | react-pdf or Puppeteer |
| Styling | Tailwind CSS |
| Email sending | Resend or Nodemailer |
| Storage (optional) | Supabase or localStorage |
| Hosting | Vercel (free tier) |

---

## What Gets Built for Each Client

- Invoice form — business details, client details, line items, tax rate
- Live PDF preview — updates as they type, shows exactly how it will look
- Brand upload — logo, primary color, font choice
- Download PDF button — clean, print-ready output
- Auto-numbered invoices (INV-001, INV-002…)
- Notes / payment terms field (bank details, due date)
